## Ingredients:







  * Ridge gourd - 1
  * Onion - 1
  * Tomato - 2.5
  * Garlic - 4
  * Curry leaves
  * Jeera - 1 tbsp
  * Coriander seeds - 1 tsp
  * Sambar powder - 2 tbsp
  * Salt
  * Oil






## Preparation:







In a pan, heat oil and add jeera, coriander seeds, garlic and curry leaves. Then, add onion and saute till the colour changes.

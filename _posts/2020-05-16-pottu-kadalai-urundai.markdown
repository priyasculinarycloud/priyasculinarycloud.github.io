## Ingredients:







  * Roasted gram dhal - 1 cup
  * Sugar - 3/4 cup
  * Cardamom powder - 2 tbsp
  * Ghee - 4 to 5 tbsp
  * Cashew nuts - 5






## Preparation:







First powder the gram dhal and sugar and mix well.
